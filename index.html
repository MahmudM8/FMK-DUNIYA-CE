Our Heritage

The FMK Duniya Story

Founded in 2010 by Farida Musa Kalla, FMK Duniya CE began as a small boutique in Kano with a vision to celebrate Northern Nigerian heritage through the finest fabrics. Today, we're a thriving women-led enterprise with four branches across Nigeria and a team of over 60 dedicated staff.

Each piece in our collection tells a story - of tradition, craftsmanship, and modern elegance. We source only the most exquisite materials, working directly with artisans to create fabrics that honor our roots while appealing to contemporary tastes.

As a proudly women-owned business, we're committed to empowering women at every level of our operations, from our skilled tailors to our management team.
